Anda Fry

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken Egg
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Pepper Powder Or Red Chillie Powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. put 1 spoonful of vegetable oil in fry pan
  2. let the frypan heat for around 3 minutes
  3. while frypan is getting heated. break the egg and put it's content in a small bowl
  4. add pepper powder and salt into the egg
  5. throw the egg from bowl onto the fry pan
  6. when you think it's ready. it's actually ready.
