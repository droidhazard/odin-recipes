Karhi

Description

Ingredients

  • Dahi
  • Besan
  • Besan ke Pakore
  • Piyaaz
  • Laal Mirch & Garam Masala

Steps

  1. pehle dahi ki lassi bana lo
  2. phir daang paka lo
  3. aur Besan Ke pakore bhi bana lo
  4. Phir daang ke andar lassi daal lo
  5. Ab iske andar Piyaz, aur Garam Masala bhi daal lo
  6. aur phir aakhir me pakore daal lo
  7. lo g karhi tayyar
