Chai

Description

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put 1 cup of milk in steel bowl
  2. add 1/4 cup water in the bowl
  3. let the milk heat for 3 minutes
  4. add Tea into it. and after few seconds add 3 spoonful of sugar into it
  5. let it heat for around 5 minutes
  6. take out a small amount of chai in the cup and check whether it needs further heating or lacks something.
  7. When you are certain the chai is ready . turn off the stove . (be sure to turn it on at first place) .
  8. drink your chai
